Past Projects

The Intersection Deck

The Intersection Deck is a card-based design tool for integrating intersectional perspectives into technology design. This system has two main components: a design methodology to generate intersectional, value-based design cards with non-designer participants whose intersectional identity facets are underrepresented in technology design, and a card-based design tool to be used by technologists within the design process.
